Problem Name: Power - Mod Power

Difficulty: Easy  
Max Score: 10

Problem Statement:
You are given three integers: a, b, and m.  
Print two lines:

1. The result of a^b  
2. The result of pow(a, b, m)

Input Format:
Three lines containing integers a, b, and m, respectively.

Constraints:
- 1 <= a <= 10  
- 1 <= b <= 10  
- 1 <= m <= 1000

Output Format:
Print the results as specified above.

Sample Input:
3  
4  
5

Sample Output:
81  
1

Explanation:
- a^b = 3^4 = 81  
- pow(3, 4, 5) = 81 % 5 = 1
